How I work

What I believe about building product

01
🔍
Research before assumptions
The best signal isn't what people say they want. It's what they actually do, and that's where I start.
02
📊
Is it worth building?
Not every problem deserves a solution. Research and data decide what makes the cut, not instinct.
03
⚙️
Think in systems
One change never stays in one place. My engineering background trained me to see how the whole thing fits together, not just the piece in front of me.
04
✨
Useful beats impressive
A product can be technically sophisticated and visually polished and still fail the only test that matters. If it doesn't help the person using it, the rest is decoration.
